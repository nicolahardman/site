My students can perform at local festivals

Performing live is an extremely important and integral practice which I encourage all my students to participate in. As a performance artist who regularly performs live, I understand how terrifying this concept can be, especially to new or more inexperienced singers. I will offer full empathic support to build self confidence, and use a microphone during sessions leading up to performances to teach the correct method. I always accompany each student during performances in a key which I tailor to suit them so that they feel both comfortable and confident.
My students have performed at several local venues and festivals such as 'Formby Live', The Formby Christmas lights switch on in the village centre and we also perform termly at Lady Green garden centre in Ince Blundell colllecting for charities. I also offer the opportunity for students to perform in annual music festivals such as the Southport Music Festival held in Meols Cop High School, and 18+ students have the option to perform in local open mic nights to gain experience and confidence.
